Frequently Asked Questions

Is this rental agreement legally valid? expand_more
Yes, the rental agreements generated on NoBroker are legally valid. When you opt for the Premium Verified service, we print it on government-issued stamp paper and facilitate notarization, making it admissible in court.
How does the E-Sign process work? expand_more
We use Aadhaar-based eSign or secure digital signatures. Both parties will receive a link to sign the document digitally via OTP verification. It's fast, secure, and legally recognized.
Can I customize the clauses? expand_more
Absolutely. Our AI Assistant suggests clauses based on your location, but you can manually add, edit, or remove any clause to suit your specific rental arrangement.
